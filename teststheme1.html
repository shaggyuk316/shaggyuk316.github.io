Welcome to the Grokox Theory Project! This site explores chaotic systems through two theories: Chaotic System Phases (Theory 1) with Binary, QuantSpark, ChaosBloom, and Orbits animations (including particles, gases, solids), and Quantum Calculations (Theory 2) with a computational tool. View animations of shapes, particles, gases, solids, and orbits, and adjust all settings in the Mixer. Also, explore GKCERN for a CERN-inspired simulation with 8 particle types. Have fun learning about chaos, quantum dynamics, and particle physics!
